## Product Overview

Our cold mix asphalt production plant is a complete turnkey solution for manufacturers looking to enter the cold patch and cold mix asphalt market. From aggregate feeding to finished bagged product, every component is engineered for reliability, consistent product quality, and ease of operation — even for operators with limited technical background.

## Production Line Components

1. **Aggregate Feeding System** — Multi-bin cold feed with variable-speed belt feeders for precise gradation control.
2. **Drying Drum** — Counter-flow design with burner, removes moisture from aggregates.
3. **Twin-Shaft Mixer** — Continuous paddle mixer with dual-liquid injection (cutback binder + additives).
4. **Bagging System** — Automatic weighing, filling, and heat-sealing for 25 kg bags.
5. **Dust Collection** — Baghouse filter system capturing >99.5% of particulate emissions.
6. **Control Room** — Climate-controlled cabin with PLC automation and recipe database.
