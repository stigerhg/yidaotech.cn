## Product Overview

Asphalt recycling agent is formulated to restore the physical properties of aged asphalt binder. Over years of service, asphalt loses light components and becomes hard, brittle, and crack-prone. Our recycling agent replenishes the binder phase, improves flexibility, and helps reclaimed asphalt pavement perform closer to a fresh mix.

The product is suitable for both hot in-place recycling and plant-mix recycling. It can be used by contractors, asphalt plants, and road authorities seeking to extend pavement life, increase RAP utilization, and reduce overall material cost.

## Where to Use

- Hot in-place recycling of aged asphalt pavement
- Plant-mix recycling with RAP
- Pavement rejuvenation programs for oxidized surfaces
- Municipal and highway rehabilitation projects
- Asphalt plants increasing RAP content in base or surface mixes

## Performance Role

1. **Soften Aged Binder** — Reduces excessive stiffness caused by oxidation.
2. **Restore Workability** — Improves coating, compaction, and mix handling.
3. **Improve Durability** — Helps reduce thermal cracking, fatigue cracking, and raveling.
4. **Support RAP Use** — Enables practical reuse of valuable reclaimed asphalt material.

## Mix Design Support

Recommended dosage depends on RAP content, aged binder condition, target penetration or viscosity, and local specifications. We can support laboratory evaluation and trial mix design to confirm the best dosage for each project.
