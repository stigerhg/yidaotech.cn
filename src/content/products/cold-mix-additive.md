## Product Overview

Our third-generation cold mix asphalt additive is a concentrated, 100% active formulation designed specifically for manufacturing premium cold mix asphalt. Unlike ordinary anti-strip agents or emulsifier-type additives on the market, this product actually modifies the asphalt binder at the molecular level — reducing the damaging effects of diesel solvent while enabling the binder to react with water for accelerated strength gain.

After more than a decade of refinement and over 10,000 tons of in-house cold mix production, this additive has proven itself in diverse climates worldwide — from Siberian winters to tropical summers.

## Key Capabilities

### 1. Proven Reliability
A decade of real-world production and application — verified on thousands of tons of cold mix used in actual road repair projects.

### 2. Wide Temperature Range
Cold mix produced with this additive remains workable at temperatures as low as -30°C. It bonds effectively even in wet potholes or extreme cold.

### 3. Long Storage Life
Cold mix manufactured with this additive can be stored for 6 months (up to 12+ months in sealed, UV-protected bags) — eliminating the waste associated with hot mix.

### 4. Rapid Traffic Opening
Repairs can be opened to traffic immediately after compaction — no waiting for curing, no lane closures.

### 5. Simple Manufacturing
Works with any forced-action mixer (batch plant, maintenance truck, mortar mixer). True cold-in, cold-out production — safe, environmentally friendly.

## How to Use

### Binder Preparation
1. Heat asphalt binder to 100–120°C (just enough for fluidity)
2. Add diesel and additive at the recommended ratio (typically 2–3% of binder weight)
3. Circulate or stir until homogeneous

### Mix Production
1. Use dry, clean alkaline aggregates
2. No special mixing equipment required — any forced-action mixer works
3. Mix until uniform coating of binder on aggregate

### Storage
- Produced cold mix can be stored in open piles or bagged
- Bagged and sealed: 12+ months storage life
- Ready for use anytime — just open and apply
