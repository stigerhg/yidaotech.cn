## Product Overview

Capsule pressure-sensitive cold patch is a smart repair material designed for cleaner placement and stronger traffic-activated performance. Instead of exposing the full binder system during storage and handling, key bonding components are protected in pressure-sensitive capsules. These capsules release under compaction from a tamper, plate compactor, or passing vehicles.

The result is a cold patch that stays cleaner in the bag, resists sticking to tools and tires, and develops strength exactly where pressure is applied. After installation, normal traffic continues compacting the repair, helping the patch become denser and more stable over time.

## Where to Use

- Urban pothole maintenance where fast reopening is required
- Utility cut reinstatement and edge-break repair
- Winter emergency repair in cold or damp conditions
- Maintenance depots that need longer stock rotation
- Export markets where shipping and storage stability matter

## How It Works

1. **Place** — Pour the material directly into the cleaned pothole. No heating, mixing, or tack coat is required.
2. **Compact** — Apply pressure with a hand tamper, vibratory plate, roller, or normal vehicle traffic.
3. **Activate** — Pressure breaks the capsules and releases the bonding phase at the aggregate contact points.
4. **Strengthen** — Continued traffic densifies the material and improves interlock without pulling binder onto tires.

- Less tracking on surrounding pavement and fewer customer complaints
