## Product Overview

Our emulsified asphalt product line covers the full spectrum of pavement preservation applications. Whether you need a rapid-setting tack coat for an overlay project, polymer-modified micro-surfacing emulsion, or a slow-setting prime coat, we manufacture to your specification with consistent quality batch after batch.

## Grade Selection Guide

| Grade | Application | Setting Speed |
|-------|-------------|---------------|
| **CSS-1** | Tack coat, prime coat, fog seal | Slow |
| **CSS-1h** | Tack coat (harder residue) | Slow |
| **CRS-2** | Chip seal, single/double surface treatment | Rapid |
| **CRS-2P** | Chip seal (polymer-modified) | Rapid |
| **CQS-1h** | Slurry seal, micro-surfacing | Quick |
| **SS-1/SS-1h** | Prime coat, dust control | Slow |

## Applications

- **Tack Coat** — Bonding layer between existing pavement and new overlay
- **Chip Seal** — Cost-effective surface treatment for rural and low-volume roads
- **Slurry Seal** — Preventive maintenance for aged asphalt surfaces
- **Micro-Surfacing** — High-performance thin overlay for rut filling and surface restoration
- **Prime Coat** — Penetrates and stabilizes granular base before asphalt paving
- **Fog Seal** — Rejuvenates oxidized pavement surfaces

## Quality Assurance

Every batch is tested in our in-house laboratory for residue content, viscosity, sieve, settlement, and particle charge before release. Certificate of Analysis (COA) provided with each shipment.
