## Product Overview

Our bituminous crack sealing tape provides the fastest solution for crack repair — literally peel, stick, and drive. Manufactured with a polymer-modified bitumen layer on a reinforced backing, this tape creates an instant waterproof seal that withstands traffic immediately after application. No special tools, no heating, no curing time.

## Best Applications

- Emergency road repairs
- Utility cut restorations
- Bridge joint temporary sealing
- Concrete expansion joints
- Warehouse and factory floor cracks
- Airport pavement temporary repairs

## Application Steps

1. **Surface Preparation** — Clean and dry the crack. Remove all dust, oil, and loose material. For best results, apply a primer coat.
2. **Cut & Peel** — Cut tape to required length. Peel release liner as you go.
3. **Apply Pressure** — Press firmly onto the crack using a hand roller or vehicle tire. Ensure full contact at edges.
4. **Done** — Ready for immediate traffic. No wait time.
