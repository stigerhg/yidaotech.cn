## Product Overview

Our permanent-grade cold mix asphalt is engineered for lasting pothole repairs in all weather conditions. Unlike traditional hot mix, it requires no heating and can be applied in wet or freezing conditions. The specially formulated binder ensures strong adhesion to the existing pavement and cures to a durable, traffic-ready surface.

## Where to Use

- Potholes on highways, streets, and rural roads
- Utility cut reinstatements
- Trench repairs after pipe laying
- Edge-break repairs
- Manhole and drain surround repairs
- Winter emergency repairs (snow and ice conditions)

## Application

1. **Clean** — Remove all loose material and standing water from the pothole. Square edges for best results.
2. **Fill** — Pour cold mix directly from the bag. Overfill by 25–30% to allow for compaction.
3. **Compact** — Compact in 50–75 mm lifts using a hand tamper or vibratory plate. Crown slightly above surrounding pavement.
4. **Open** — Traffic-ready immediately after compaction. No curing time required.

## Economic Advantages

- Eliminates hot mix plant dependency for small repairs
- One-person operation — reduces crew costs
- No waste — reseal unused material and use for next job
- Year-round availability — no seasonal shutdown for winter repairs
