## Product Overview

Color pavement sealcoat combines ultra-thin anti-skid surfacing with decorative pavement coloring. It is designed for projects that need both functional skid resistance and clear visual guidance, such as bike lanes, pedestrian areas, bus lanes, intersections, school zones, landscape roads, and parking areas.

The coating is applied at only 0.5–1.5 mm, reducing material consumption and construction time compared with thick colored overlays. The polymer-modified binder bonds firmly to asphalt and concrete, while UV-stable pigments keep the surface bright and consistent under outdoor exposure.

## Combined Anti-Skid and Decorative Function

This product merges two previous color pavement systems: ultra-thin anti-skid pavement and color sprayed pavement coating. Contractors can use it as a simple color restoration coating, or broadcast anti-skid aggregate into the wet film for higher friction requirements on slopes, curves, ramps, and intersections.

## Where to Use

- Bike lanes, greenways, and pedestrian paths
- Bus lanes, taxi lanes, and traffic guidance zones
- Crosswalks, intersections, and school safety areas
- Landscape roads, parks, and scenic area pavement
- Color restoration of faded asphalt or concrete pavement
- Ramps and slopes requiring additional anti-skid texture

## Application

1. **Prepare Surface** — Clean dust, oil, loose particles, and standing water from the substrate.
2. **Mask Edges** — Protect curbs, markings, drains, and adjacent pavement.
3. **Apply Coating** — Spray or squeegee the coating at the specified rate. Add anti-skid aggregate when required.
4. **Cure** — Allow 1–3 hours before opening to traffic, depending on temperature and film thickness.

## Key Advantages

- Thin, fast, and economical compared with colored asphalt replacement
- Multiple colors for traffic guidance and decorative design
- High friction texture available without thick overlay construction
- Compatible with small repairs, full lanes, and decorative pavement zones
- Practical export packaging and simple job-site application

## 沥青彩衣产品介绍

### 产品定位

沥青彩衣是一种用于彩色路面修复与美化升级的水性环保彩色沥青涂装工艺，可在原有沥青路面基础上进行快速换装，兼顾色彩表现、耐磨防滑、附着力与施工效率。

**核心特性：** 水性环保（无明显异味）| 防滑耐磨 | 不易褪色 | 适配沥青路面

针对传统彩色路面易氧化、磨损、褪色，以及旧路拆除工程量大、浪费明显等问题，沥青彩衣提供一种更轻量的路面美化与功能提升方案。

适用于非机动车步行路、景观道路、自行车道、校园操场、广场彩化、球场车道、停车场等慢行交通及景观空间。

采用底中面三层结构，由亲油基层、中涂耐磨层、罩面保护层组成。可配合薄层沥青磨耗层形成"2cm 薄层沥青 + 沥青彩衣"的整体方案。

### 核心优势

- **粘结好** — 与沥青基面相容，黑沥青完成后即可施工，减少等待时间。
- **柔韧不易起皮** — 特殊材料体系兼具柔韧性与强度，降低脱落风险。
- **防滑耐磨** — 有厚度、耐磨耗，雨天行车不打滑，适合慢行系统使用。
- **水性环保** — 水性材料，无 VOC 排放，施工环境更友好。
- **色彩纯正** — 无高温不变色，色泽饱满自然，不易褪色。
- **经济高效** — 相较厚层彩色沥青重铺，方案更轻量，适合旧路快速焕新。

### 适用场景

- 城市绿道、慢行步道、自行车道
- 公园景观路、景区道路、滨水步道
- 校园、操场、广场、社区活动空间
- 停车场、球场车道、道路节点美化
- 黑色沥青路面的改色、修复与景观升级
