## Overview

Our technology licensing program gives you everything needed to start manufacturing cold mix asphalt — without years of trial and error. We transfer our proven formulas, raw material sourcing network, production know-how, and quality control systems so you can launch your own branded product line in your market.

## What's Included

### Phase 1: Formula Transfer (Week 1-4)
- Five proprietary cold mix formulations (standard, premium, winter-grade, driveway-grade, utility-cut-grade)
- Complete bill of materials with substitute options for local sourcing
- Mixing sequence and process control parameters

### Phase 2: Production Setup (Week 5-8)
- Production line layout and equipment specifications
- Raw material supplier introduction and sample evaluation
- Pilot batch production with our engineer on-site

### Phase 3: Quality Control (Week 9-10)
- QC laboratory equipment list and setup guide
- Testing SOPs (Marshall stability, workability, coating, stripping)
- Product certification support (local standards compliance)

### Phase 4: Market Launch (Week 11-12)
- Packaging design templates
- Technical data sheets and marketing materials
- Go-to-market strategy consultation

## Ongoing Support (License Period)

- Monthly formula optimization calls
- New product R&D — access to our latest developments
- Trouble-shooting hotline (24-hour response)
- Annual on-site audit and training refresh

## Who This Is For

- Existing asphalt or construction materials companies expanding into cold mix
- Entrepreneurs entering the road maintenance market
- Government agencies establishing local production capability
- Distributors seeking backward integration
