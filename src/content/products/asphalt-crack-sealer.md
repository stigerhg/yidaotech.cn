## Product Overview

Our commercial-grade asphalt crack sealer is formulated for professional pavement maintenance contractors. 
It provides a durable, flexible seal that prevents water intrusion and extends pavement life.

Ideal for highways, parking lots, airport runways, and commercial driveways. 
Apply with standard hot-applied crack sealing equipment.

## Benefits

- Reduces pavement maintenance costs by preventing crack expansion
- Extends pavement service life by 3-5 years when properly applied
- Meets ASTM D6690 specifications
