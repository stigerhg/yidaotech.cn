## Product Overview

Color cold patch asphalt solves the longstanding challenge of repairing colored asphalt pavements. Traditional colored asphalt is produced in large batches at central mixing plants and laid with专业 pavers — making small repairs (just a few square meters) impractical. This leaves colored pavements with unsightly "black patches" where regular black cold mix was used as a temporary fix.

Shining Road Technology developed our color cold patch formulation in 2017, combining over a decade of black cold mix expertise with specialized color-stabilizing technology. The result is a product that matches the original pavement color while delivering the same all-weather performance as our standard cold mix.

## Where to Use

- Color pavement pothole and crack repairs
- Bike lanes, jogging paths, and pedestrian walkways
- Landscape road color restoration
- Colored asphalt surface patching
- Decorative pavement maintenance

## Application

1. **Clean** — Remove all loose debris and standing water from the repair area. Square the edges for best results.
2. **Fill** — Pour color cold patch directly from the bag. Overfill slightly (10–15%) to allow for compaction.
3. **Compact** — Tamp firmly using a hand tamper or vibratory plate. Smooth to match surrounding surface level.
4. **Cure** — Allow extended curing time compared to standard cold patch before opening to heavy traffic.
