## Product Overview

Our commercial-grade hot-applied asphalt crack sealer is formulated for professional pavement maintenance contractors. Manufactured using high-quality polymers, plasticizers, and asphalt base, it delivers a durable, flexible, and waterproof seal that prevents moisture penetration into the pavement base — extending service life by 3–5 years when properly applied.

## Key Applications

- **Highways & Expressways** — transverse, longitudinal, and block cracking
- **Parking Lots** — routine crack sealing maintenance for commercial lots
- **Airport Runways & Taxiways** — meets aviation authority fuel-resistance requirements
- **Bridge Decks** — flexible enough to accommodate structural movement
- **Municipal Roads** — cost-effective preventive maintenance at scale

## Application Guidelines

1. **Crack Preparation** — Clean and dry the crack using compressed air, heat lance, or router. Remove all debris, moisture, and loose material.
2. **Heating** — Heat sealant in an oil-jacketed melter with mechanical agitation to 180–205°C. Avoid overheating to prevent polymer degradation.
3. **Application** — Apply with a wand or pour pot. Fill crack to flush or slightly recessed level (3–5 mm below surface for overbanding).
4. **Curing** — Allow 15–30 minutes before opening to traffic (depending on ambient temperature).

- Compliant with ASTM, AASHTO, and international standards
