## Product Overview

Over time, colored asphalt pavements oxidize and fade under sun exposure and traffic wear. Traditional solutions require removing and reinstalling the pavement — costly, wasteful, and disruptive. Our color sprayed asphalt coating provides an efficient, economical alternative: simply spray over the existing surface to restore vibrant color or change pavement color entirely.

Developed by Shining Road Technology using advanced acrylic-polymer and waterproof coating technologies, this product creates a durable, UV-resistant color layer that bonds firmly to both asphalt and concrete substrates.

## Applications

- Color restoration of faded colored asphalt pavements
- Pavement color change (e.g., converting asphalt to colored surface)
- Landscape roads, bike paths, and pedestrian walkways
- Crosswalks and intersection color highlighting
- Decorative pavement projects

## Application Method

1. **Surface Preparation** — Clean the pavement surface thoroughly. Remove all dirt, oil stains, and loose particles. Allow to dry.
2. **Masking** — Protect adjacent areas that should not receive color coating.
3. **Apply** — Spray using standard paint spray equipment at 0.3–0.8 kg/m². One coat is typically sufficient.
4. **Cure** — Allow 2 hours to cure before opening to traffic.

## Key Benefits

- **Fast** — 2-hour cure time means minimal traffic disruption
- **Economical** — Low material consumption (0.3–0.8 kg/m²)
- **Versatile** — Can change pavement color completely or restore faded color
- **Durable** — Resists UV, water, and wear for 3–5 years
- **Simple** — No specialized equipment beyond standard spray gear
