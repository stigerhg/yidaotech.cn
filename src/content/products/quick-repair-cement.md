## Product Overview

Our quick repair cement mortar is a thin-layer, fast-setting repair material designed for cement concrete pavement rehabilitation. With a 15-minute initial set and traffic-ready strength in just 1 hour, it is ideal for emergency repairs and high-traffic areas where minimizing lane closure time is critical.

The material's bond strength exceeds the tensile strength of ordinary concrete — pull tests consistently show failure in the base concrete rather than at the repair interface. Its near-zero shrinkage and thermal expansion matching ensure durable, long-lasting repairs.

## Applications

- Concrete pavement surface spalling and scaling
- Delamination repairs on airport runways and taxiways
- Highway ramp edge and corner repairs
- Gas station and truck stop forecourt repairs (rapid return to service)
- Bridge deck thin-layer repairs
- Freeze-thaw and de-icing salt damage restoration

## Performance Characteristics

1. **Fast Setting & High Strength** — 15-minute initial set, 1 hour before traffic opening
2. **Superior Bond** — Flexural strength exceeds C30 concrete; pull tests break base concrete, not the repair
3. **Dimensional Stability** — Near-zero drying shrinkage prevents edge gap formation
4. **Thermal Compatibility** — Expansion coefficient nearly identical to ordinary concrete, preventing thermal stress failure
5. **Durability** — Excellent freeze-thaw and chemical resistance

## Application Method

1. **Prepare** — Remove all loose and deteriorated concrete. Saw-cut edges for a clean repair boundary. Clean and saturate the surface with water (SSD condition).
2. **Mix** — Add water per instructions and mix to a stiff, workable consistency.
3. **Apply** — Place and compact firmly into the repair area. Screed flush with surrounding surface.
4. **Finish** — Trowel smooth and apply curing compound or wet cure.
5. **Open** — Traffic-ready after 1 hour at 20°C.
