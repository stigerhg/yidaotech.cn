## Product Overview

Our cold pour crack filler is a solvent-based, ready-to-use formulation designed for small to medium pavement cracks. It requires no heating or special application equipment — simply pour, let it self-level, and allow to cure. Ideal for property managers, homeowners, and light commercial contractors.

## Best For

- Residential and commercial driveways
- Small parking lots
- Footpaths and bike paths
- Tennis courts and recreational surfaces
- Pre-treatment before sealcoating

## How to Use

1. **Clean** — Remove all debris, vegetation, and loose material from the crack using a wire brush or compressed air.
2. **Apply** — Pour directly from the container into the crack. Slightly overfill to allow for shrinkage during curing.
3. **Cure** — Allow 24 hours to cure at 20°C before opening to traffic. Colder temperatures may require longer cure time.

## Advantages Over Hot-Applied

- Zero equipment investment — suitable for small maintenance budgets
- Safe to use — no burn risk or fire hazard
- One-person operation — no crew required
- Long shelf life — store up to 12 months in sealed container
