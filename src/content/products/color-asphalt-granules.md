## Product Overview

Color asphalt granules provide a simple, cost-effective solution for producing colored asphalt in any standard mixing plant. Rather than installing dedicated color-dosing equipment or purchasing pre-colored binder, simply add these granules directly to the pugmill during the mixing cycle.

Each granule contains a precisely formulated blend of color pigments and a compatible binder carrier. When mixed with hot aggregate and asphalt binder, the granules melt and disperse uniformly — producing a consistent, vibrant colored asphalt mix.

## Best For

- Small to medium colored asphalt paving projects
- Municipal bike lanes, pedestrian zones, and decorative paving
- Short-run colored asphalt production (avoiding minimum batch constraints)
- Emergency color restoration work

## How to Use

1. Add granules directly to the pugmill at 7–8% of total mix weight
2. Follow standard mixing cycle for hot mix asphalt
3. Inspect for uniform color distribution before discharge
4. Lay and compact using standard asphalt paving equipment
