## Expanding Access to Cold Mix Technology

Shining Road Technology is pleased to announce the official launch of our **Cold Mix Asphalt Technology Licensing Program**, now available to partners in Southeast Asia, Africa, the Middle East, and Latin America.

For years, high-quality cold mix asphalt has been dominated by a handful of multinational suppliers. Our licensing program changes that — giving local manufacturers the formulas, raw material network, production know-how, and ongoing R&D support to produce competitive cold mix products in their own market.

## What Partners Receive

- **Five base formulas** covering standard, premium, winter-grade, and specialty applications
- **Raw material supplier database** with pre-negotiated pricing across multiple regions
- **On-site training** by our senior production engineers (2-week program)
- **QC laboratory setup** including equipment specifications, SOPs, and testing protocols
- **Ongoing support** — monthly formula optimization, new product R&D, and trouble-shooting hotline

> "We don't just sell you a formula and walk away. We stay with you through the entire journey — from your first pilot batch to full-scale commercial production."

## Eligible Regions

The program is currently accepting applications from:
- Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam, Thailand, Malaysia
- Kenya, Nigeria, Ghana, South Africa, Tanzania
- UAE, Saudi Arabia, Oman
- Brazil, Mexico, Colombia

Contact our technology transfer team for a detailed program prospectus and territory availability.
