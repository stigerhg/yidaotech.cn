## Milestone Delivery

Earlier this month, our engineering team completed the factory acceptance test (FAT) for a **30 TPH cold mix asphalt production plant** destined for Nairobi, Kenya — our largest single-line delivery to date.

The plant was purchased by a leading Kenyan construction materials supplier who identified a growing demand for year-round pothole repair materials across East Africa's expanding road network.

## Plant Specifications

- **Capacity**: 30 tons per hour (single shift: ~200 tons/day)
- **Configuration**: 4-bin cold feed, counter-flow dryer, twin-shaft mixer, automatic bagging line
- **Products**: 3 cold mix grades from a single production line
- **Control**: Siemens PLC with remote diagnostics capability
- **Delivery**: 8 × 40' HC containers

## Beyond the Equipment

This project includes our full technology licensing package:
- 5 cold mix formulations adapted for East African aggregate types and climate
- 2-week operator training program for 6 staff members
- QC laboratory setup with equipment, SOPs, and certification support
- 12-month remote technical support with quarterly performance reviews

## Growing African Demand

Infrastructure investment across Africa is driving demand for road maintenance materials. The African Development Bank estimates that the continent requires $130–170 billion annually for infrastructure development. Cold mix asphalt — with its low equipment barrier and year-round usability — is uniquely suited to this market.

We expect to deliver 3–4 additional plants to the region over the next 18 months.
